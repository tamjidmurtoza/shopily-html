How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our products, select the items you wish to purchase, and add them to your cart. Once you're ready, proceed to checkout, where you can enter your shipping details, select a payment method, and complete your purchase.

Can I modify or cancel my order after it's been placed?

Orders can only be modified or canceled within the first hour after placement. After this period, the order begins processing, and changes cannot be made. If you need to make changes, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website to track your order's status.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major visa & master Debit/Credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer installment payment options through selected providers.

What should I do if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order. We will arrange for a replacement or issue a refund.

What should I do if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order. We will arrange for a replacement or issue a refund.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and select mobile banking services for a smooth checkout experience.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All transactions are encrypted using SSL technology and processed through trusted, secure payment gateways to keep your personal and financial information safe.

When will my payment be charged?

Your payment is charged immediately after you confirm your order at checkout. In some cases, it may appear as a pending charge until your bank fully processes it.

Do you offer cash on delivery (COD)?

Yes, we offer cash on delivery for selected locations. You can check the availability of COD for your area during the checkout process.

Why was my payment declined?

Payments may be declined due to incorrect card details, insufficient funds, or bank restrictions. Please verify your details or contact your bank for assistance, then try again.

Can I get a refund to a different payment method?

Refunds are always issued to the original payment method used for the purchase to ensure security and accuracy. Please allow 5–10 business days for the amount to appear in your account.

How can I find current promotions or discounts?

You can find all active promotions and discounts on our homepage, promotional banners, or by subscribing to our newsletter for exclusive deals and early access to sales.

How do I apply a promo code at checkout?

To apply a promo code, enter it in the “Discount Code” or “Promo Code” box during checkout and click “Apply.” The discount will be reflected in your total amount instantly.

Why is my promo code not working?

Promo codes may not work if they’ve expired, reached their usage limit, or don’t apply to the products in your cart. Please check the promotion’s terms and conditions for details.

Can I use multiple promo codes on one order?

Most promotions allow only one promo code per order. However, some offers may combine automatically during checkout if they’re eligible for stacking.

Do you offer discounts for first-time customers?

Yes! First-time customers can enjoy a special welcome discount. Simply sign up for our newsletter or create an account to receive your unique promo code via email.

What happens if I return an item purchased with a discount?

If you return an item bought with a discount or promo code, the refund will reflect the amount paid after the discount was applied. Promo codes are not reissued once used.

What shipping options do you offer?

We offer standard and express shipping options to most locations. Shipping methods and delivery times are displayed during checkout based on your address and selected service.

How long will delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days for standard shipping and 1–3 business days for express shipping. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and order volume.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to selected countries. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times are calculated at checkout based on the destination.

How can I track my shipment?

Once your order has been shipped, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can track your shipment directly from our website or through the courier’s portal.

What should I do if my package is delayed?

If your delivery is delayed, please check your tracking details for updates. You can also contact our customer service team, and we’ll help you locate your package as soon as possible.

What happens if my order arrives damaged or missing items?

If your package arrives damaged or with missing items, contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery. We’ll arrange a replacement or issue a refund promptly.

How do I become a seller on Shopily?

To start selling on Shopily, create a seller account by registering with your business details, contact information, and bank account. Once approved, you can start listing your products immediately.

Is there a fee to sell on Shopily?

Shopily offers flexible selling plans. There’s no cost to create an account, but a small commission is charged per sale. Additional premium tools and advertising options are available for professional sellers.

How do I list my products?

You can list products easily from your seller dashboard. Add high-quality images, clear descriptions, pricing, and stock quantity. Products go live once reviewed and approved by the Shopily team.

How do I receive payments for my sales?

Payments are transferred directly to your registered bank account after each successful order. Depending on your payment cycle, funds are typically released within 3–7 business days after order completion.

Who handles shipping and delivery?

You can choose between self-shipping or Shopily’s fulfillment service. If you use Shopily Fulfillment, we handle packaging, delivery, and tracking for a small fee per order.

Can I promote my products on Shopily?

Yes! Shopily offers advertising and featured placement options to help increase visibility and sales. You can run campaigns directly from your seller dashboard to reach more customers.

What is Shopily's return policy?

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided they are in original condition with tags attached. Some categories like perishable goods, intimate apparel, and personalized items may be exempt from returns.

How do I initiate a return?

Log into your Shopily account, go to "My Orders" and select the item you wish to return. Follow the prompts to generate a return label and shipping instructions. You'll receive email confirmation once processed.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your returned item. The timing for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution.

Who pays for return shipping?

Return shipping is free for items that arrived damaged, defective, or incorrect. For other returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the seller offers a prepaid return label.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Exchanges are available for select items and depend on seller policies. Check the product page or contact the seller directly to inquire about exchange options for your specific item.

What if my item arrives damaged or defective?

Contact Shopily customer service immediately with photos of the damaged item and packaging. We'll arrange a free return and full refund or replacement, depending on your preference and item availability.
