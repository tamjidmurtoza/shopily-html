About Us

Shopily is a multivendor e-commerce platform that connects buyers and sellers in a seamless, secure marketplace. We provide vendors with easy-to-use tools to grow their businesses, while offering customers a wide range of products across various categories. At Shopily, we’re committed to delivering a reliable and enjoyable shopping experience for everyone. Join us and discover quality products from trusted sellers all in one place.

Who We Are

Shopily is a multivendor e-commerce platform connecting buyers and sellers, offering a wide range of quality products with a seamless, secure shopping experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple to help sellers grow their businesses while giving shoppers an easy, secure, and enjoyable experience with tons of great products.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a global leader in multivendor e-commerce, connecting businesses and customers worldwide through innovation, trust, and exceptional service.

At Shopily, our 'Day 1' mentality means approaching everything with the energy and entrepreneurial drive of a startup, as if it's our very first day in business.

Earning and maintaining our customers' trust is the primary focus of our 'Day 1' approach. Our decision-making process encourages employees to evaluate whether an action is a one-way door—significant and difficult to reverse—or a two-way door, allowing for easier changes and course corrections. Learn more about our journey and values in our Annual Letters to Shareholders from 2024 to the present.
